Inquisitive Quiz

Even though your brain don’t have pain receptors but you still get headaches! BECAUSE of these BRAIN TEASERS. GODDD!! Why do they even exist?

Inquisitive is an annual online puzzle rush organized by BVPCSI to tickle your brain and truly announce your genius to the world.

It is one thing to know your worth and another to prove it. Competitions like Inquisitive not only gives you the platform to do so but also to get the taste of the competition that's around.

Such events not only tell you your strengths but also expose your weaknesses, and as someone said knowledge of your limitations is the true mark of a leader.

But let’s not get ahead of ourselves, one can always learn and yearn to do better and be better, and participating in such events can really make the difference.

One of the main competencies that any company today should look for in any new employee is inquisitiveness. If you are looking to build a stable of expat-eligible employees, do yourself a favor and move inquisitive to the top of the list.
