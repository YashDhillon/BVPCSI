Marvel VS DCEU

There are two kind of people in this world, one who follow the Heroes, the Marvel fanatics and the other who follow the Villains, the DC fiends.

BVPCSI organized the Marvel VS DCEU Quiz in collaboration with its very own BVPCSI QAL.

Today we live in the golden era when we get to see the heroes we love and cherish in live-action and believable stories. From the early Superman movies to the fantastic MCU the expectations and quality has only risen in these stories and the storytelling.

Today the fantasies are more believable, culturing a fanbase of fanatics.

Did you grow up reading comic books by the pound? If so, you probably consider yourself a connoisseur when it comes to superheroes. However, only a true fan will be able to pass this quiz with flying colors, so you better be ready to put your money where your mouth is.

It's safe to say that if you don't know if Superman is a DC or Marvel character, you'll have a lot of trouble while trying to pass this quiz. However, there's nothing to be ashamed about if you don't, it just means that you aren't a nerd.

With the overwhelming response at the orientation, the BVPCSI - Quizzing and Literature (QAL) Chapter organized a much awaited and in a way demanded, The Marvel vs DC Extended Universe Quiz. Gentry coming from all over the city tells not only about the success of the event but also the popularity of the QAL Chapter in the Delhi Quizzing Circuit.
