Introduction to Programming

Ever noticed the scale of technology that we have surrounded ourselves with now as compared to that of 5 years ago?

Coding Ninjas in collaboration with BVPCSI organized a workshop on Introduction to Programming. The workshop witnessed enormous participation and set things in order for the newcomers.

Coding ninjas in collaboration with BVPCSI conducted the workshop Introduction to programming.

The aim of the workshop was to give students a basic introduction of programming.

Various basic concepts were touched upon to give the students a wholesome view of the programming world.

Concepts like scope of different programming languages, utility of languages, data types and many more were touched upon.

Over 100 students took part in the event making the workshop a grand success.
